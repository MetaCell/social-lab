You will be randomly and anonymously paired with another participant or a bot.

One of you will be selected at random to be participant A; the other will be participant B. You will learn whether you are participant A or B prior to making any decision.

To start, participant A receives {{ Constants.amount_allocated }}; participant B receives nothing. Participant A can send some or all of his {{ Constants.amount_allocated }} to participant B. Before B receives these points they will be multiplied by {{ Constants.multiplication_factor }}. Once B receives the tripled points he can decide to send some or all of his points to A, or to keep them all for himself.

At the very end of the game you will be asked to guess if you played against a human or a bot partner.
