In this task, you will play against a partner. The partner could be a human or could be the computer. Your job is gather as many points as possible, while trying to work out which you are playing against.

The game will have several rounds. In each round, you will be asked to send a message about your intentions to your partner - whether you intend to declare peace or wage war. Each of you then will simultaneously and privately chooses whether you actually declare peace or war. Your payoffs will be determined by the choices of both as below:

The Other Participant
Peace War
You Peace {{ Constants.both_cooperate_payoff }}, {{ Constants.both_cooperate_payoff }} 0 point, {{ Constants.betray_payoff }}
War {{ Constants.betray_payoff }}, 0 point {{ Constants.both_defect_payoff }}, {{ Constants.both_defect_payoff }}

In each cell, the amount (in points) to the left is the payoff for you and to the right for the other participant.

At the end you will be asked to guess if you played against a human or a bot opponent.
