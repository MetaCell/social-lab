In this task, you will play against a partner in a game where you have the chance to communicate your intentions before making a decision about whether to wage war or declare peace. Your partner could be a human or a computer. Your job is to win as much money as possible and work out which type of partner you think you are playing against.

The game will have several rounds. For each round, you will first be asked to send a message about your intentions - whether you intend to start a war, or declare peace. This is just to communicate with your partner and you don't have to stick to your stated intentions.

After you have stated your intentions, each of you then will then make your decision: to actually wage war or declare peace.

  • If you both choose peace, you both win 10 cents.
  • If one person chooses peace, but the other chooses war, the person who chooses war wins 15 cents and the person who chooses peace wins nothing.
  • If you both choose war, you win 5 cents each.

After each round you will be asked to rate whether you think you are playing with a human or a computer.
