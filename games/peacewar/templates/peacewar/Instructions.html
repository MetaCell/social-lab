In this task, you will play 5 rounds against a partner in a game where you decide to declare peace or make war against them.

The game will have several rounds. For each round, you will first be asked to communicate your intentions - whether you intend to start a war, or declare peace.

Each of you then will then make your real decision: to actually wage war or declare peace. You can stick with your stated intentions or change your mind.

  • If you both choose peace, you both win 10 cents.
  • If one person chooses peace, but the other chooses war, the person who chooses war wins 15 cents and the person who chooses peace wins nothing.
  • If you both choose war, you win 5 cents each.

You will play 5 rounds against the same partner, so you can earn up to an additional 75 cents bonus.

After each round, you will be asked to rate what you think motivated their decision.

Your partner could be a human or a computer. Your job is to earn as much money as possible and you will be asked at the end which type of partner you think you are playing against.
