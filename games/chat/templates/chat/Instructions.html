You are about to start a chat. You will be assigned to either a fellow human or a computer chatbot. Your job is to work out which you are communicating with.

You will be asked every few lines to rate whether you think you are interacting with a human or a chatbot using a sliding scale. We analyse the text, so please don't use abbreviations or slang and please do not use offensive words or text or we will reject the task. After writing, please wait for the other person to respond before writing your response.

You MUST use a desktop web browser. This will not work if you use a mobile web browser.

It can take up to a minute to find a computer or human partner, so please wait after clicking. Good luck!
